Dr. Carolina Montalvo, PhD

Dr. Carolina Montalvo is a psychologist who specializes in administering ketamine-assisted psychotherapy (KAP).  Ketamine is a medication that is commonly used as an anesthetic in surgical procedures. In recent years. leading researchers and clinicians have been studying and utilizing Ketamine in lower doses to treat mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).
Dr. Montalvo helps her clients prepare to work with ketamine via prep sessions i.e., expectation setting & harm reduction), provides safety and support while under the effects of Ketamine, and integrates the insights gained through this process to create a richer and more meaningful daily existence.

Kamila Veljkovic, CNS

My name is Kamila Veljkovic, I am Certified Nutrition Specialist®,  Founder and Lead Nutritionist of Complete Health for Women, a virtual women’s clinic where I specialize in helping women with autoimmune disease and general health issues to reverse their conditions through holistic nutrition and lifestyle strategies.
As a holistic nutritionist, I believe that the key to reversing autoimmune disease lies in addressing the root cause of the disease.
